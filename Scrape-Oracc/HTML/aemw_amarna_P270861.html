(001) Speak to Intaruta, the ruler of Achshaph, the message of the king: "I herewith send this tablet to you, saying to you, "Guard! May you guard the places of the king that are in your care."

(006) The king herewith sends to you Hanni, the king's stable master in Canaan. Whatever he says to you, obey it very carefully lest the king find guilt in you. Very carefully obey and perform every word that he says to you. And guard! Guard! Do not be negligent. And may you prepare for the arrival of the king's troops: there is much food, there is much wine and everything (else. Now, he will arrive very soon and cut off the head of the king's enemies.
