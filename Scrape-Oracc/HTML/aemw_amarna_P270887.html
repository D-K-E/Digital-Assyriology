(062) As you sent the words of my father to me -- never mind! Don't say his words! Moreovoer, "Establish a partnership (and) alliance between us!" that which you wrote, these are your words. Now, you and I, the both of us, are partners, so I became angry with your messengers because they say to you, "Nothing is given to we who go to the land of Egypt." Of those who come here to see me, does one of the two leave but he has not taken silver, gold, oil, garments, everything (and) anything good, more than would be from another country? But he says nothing true to the one who is sending him! The first time, messengers went to your father, and their mouths were telling lies. They went a second time, and they were telling the lies to you. So I said, "Whether [I g]ive something to them or I do not give (it) to them, they are <te>lling l[ie]s either way," and so I decided about them: I did not give anything else to them.
