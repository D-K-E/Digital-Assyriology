(013) Now, I heard that you have built a new house (text: houses). Now, I am sending something to you, supplies for your house, and, now, I am preparing something else, many things, for the arrival of your messenger who will take away your daughter. If your messenger returns, then I will send them to you. Now, I have sent the greeting-gift for the new house under the supervision of Šutti: 1 ebony bed, inlaid with ivory; 3 ebony bed(s), inlaid with gold; 1 ebony head-rest, inlaid with gold; 1 large ebony chair, inlaid with gold; 5 ebony chair(s), inlaid with gold; 4 ebony chair(s), inlaid with gold. Regarding these things, all of the gold, the weight (is) 7 minas (and) 9 shekels in gold. The weight of the silver (is) 1 mina (and) 8 shekels of sil[ver] and 1/2 shekels of silver. 10 ebony footstool(s); [...] 1 ebony qablu, inlaid with gold; [... #] ebony footstool(s), inlaid with gold; [...].
