(008) My brother, herewith I am sending my messenger with your messenger into the land of Egypt. Now, I am sending 500 (talents) of copper to you -- I am sending (it) to you as my brother's greeting-gift. Do not become angry about the copper because (the amount) is small. Now see, in my land, the hand of Nergal, my lord, killed all the men of my land, so there is no copper worker. So may you not be angry, my brother.
