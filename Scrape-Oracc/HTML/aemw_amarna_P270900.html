(039) Moreover, speak to Ya<n>hamu: "You see, Rib-Haddi is in your control; whatever [ha]ppens to him is your responsibility, lest the expeditionary force attack me." And I likewise write to him: "If thus you don't speak, then I will abandon the city and I will bail. What is more, if you do not send word back to me, then I will (still) abandon the city and I, along with those loyal to me, will (still) bail. So be warned."
