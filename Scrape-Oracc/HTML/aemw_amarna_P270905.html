(031) Furthermore, why does the king compare me to the city rulers? When it comes to the city rulers, they have their cities; their wor<ki>ng class is in their charge. But, as for me, Aziru owns my cities and he is seeking my (life). Why should I make an alliance with him? Who are the do[gs], the sons of Abdi-Aširt[a], that they should act however they want and send the king's cities up in flames?
