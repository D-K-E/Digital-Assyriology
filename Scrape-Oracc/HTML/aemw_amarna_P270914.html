(001) To the king, my lord my god, my sun god, the message of Abi-milku, your servant: Seven (times) and seven times I fell at the feet of the king, my lord. I am the dust underneath the sandals of the king, my lord.

(005) My lord is the sun god who day by day comes forth over all regions in accordance with the custom of the sun god, his gracious father, who gives life by his sweet breath and returns with his north wind, by whom all the regions are established in peace by the power of (his) arm, who gives his voice in the heavens like Baʾlu, so that the all the regions are terrified at his cry.
