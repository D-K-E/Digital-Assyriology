(006) Look, I am diligently guarding the king's city which he entrusted to me. My intention is to go in order to see the face of the king, my lord, but due to the hand of Zimri-Yidda (of) Sidon I am unable. He heard that I was entering (Egypt) so he waged war against me. May the king give 20 men to guard the city of the king, my lord, so that I may enter the presence of the king, my lord, in order to see his gracious face. I have turned to the beautiful appearance of the king, my lord. May the k[ing], my lord, ask his commissioner if I have turned to the presence of the king, my lord.
