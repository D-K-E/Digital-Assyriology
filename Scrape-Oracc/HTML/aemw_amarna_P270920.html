(011) And the king, my lord, has spoken regarding Hanʾi. My lord, I was in Tunip and did not know that he had arrived. I went up after him as soon as I heard, but I did not catch up with him. May Hanʾi arrive in safety so that the king, my lord, may question him about how I looked after him. My brothers and Bêt-ʾili went to assist him. They provided him (with large) cattle, small cattle, and fowl, (as well as) his food (and) drink.
