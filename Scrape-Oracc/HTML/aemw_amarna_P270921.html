(016) My lord, the whole land is afraid in the face of your troops and chariotry. If my lord would seize (control of) this land to (be) his own land, then in this year may my lord dispatch his troops and chariotry and let them come here, because the whole land of Nuǵašše (belongs) to my lord. My lord, if the troops would have come forth (and) taken a stand in Amurru for (a mere) six days, then they would certainly have taken Aziru.
