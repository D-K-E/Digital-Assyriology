(018) Moreover, since Haya says [to the ki]ng, "If we do not [move out] to Amurru, then [the men of] Tyre, Sidon, and Beirut will bring (him)," whose cities are these? Do they not belong to the king? Appoint one man in each city and let him not allow an Amurru ship (to depart), so that they will assassinate Abdi-Aširta. (It was) the king, not they themselves, (who) placed him over them. May the king speak to the three cities and the ships of the naval fleet that they do not move out to Amurru. Seize Abdi-Aširta or may he be handed over to you. Take note of the affairs of your faithful servant.
