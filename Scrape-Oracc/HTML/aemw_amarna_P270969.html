(041) Moreover, take note of the deeds of Peʾya, son of Gulat[i], against Gezer, the maidservant of the king, my lord. How long has he been plundering it so that it is treated like a damaged pot on account of him. From the mountains people are ransomed for thirty (shekels of) silver, but from Peʾya (people are ransomed) for one hundred (shekels of) silver! So be informed of these affairs of your "servant!"
