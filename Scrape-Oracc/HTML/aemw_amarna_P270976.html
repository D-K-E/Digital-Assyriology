⸢DUMU⸣.MEŠ ši-ip-ri i-na ṣe-ti

(r 043) Why are messengers always being made to stand under the sun and then dying under the sun? If there is some benefit to (a messenger's) standing under the sun for the king, then let him stand and die under the sun. Let there be a benefit for the king, though! But if there is not, why are they dying under the sun? Are the messengers that we se[nd back and forth] -- or (now) a second set of messengers -- kept alive? They are made to die under the sun!
