(o 007) I have sent my messenger to you in order to see you and to see your land. Until now, my ancestors did not send (messengers). Now, I have sent (a messenger) to you. I am sending 1 fine chariot, 2 horses, and 1 "date-bead" of genuine lapis lazuli (lit. lapis lazuli from the mountains) for your greeting gift.
