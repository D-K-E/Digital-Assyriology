(009) May the king know that Byblos, the loyal maidservant of the king, is intact, but the hostility of the apiru forces against me is very severe. So may the king, my lord, not keep silent about Ṣumur lest ev[eryo]ne ally with the apiru forces. (It is only) by means of the king's commissioner, who is in the Ṣumur, (that) Gubla survives.
