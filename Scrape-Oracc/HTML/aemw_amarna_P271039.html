(001) Speak to the king of the land Egypt, my brother: Message of the king of the land of Alašiya, your brother. It is well for me. May it be well for you. May it be very well for your household, your primary wives, your horses, your chariots and among the numerous people of your troops, your regions, (and) your senior officials.
