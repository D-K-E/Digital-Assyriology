(004) To the king, my lord: May he be aware that my [men] serve Milk-ilu. What have I done to Milk-ilu that he should rob my men on account of his own servants? He gave his own servants to Tagi, his father-in-law. What can I myself do, since I am (apparently) not a loyal servant of the king? Are my men [un]able to [focus] their attention on me? [So] may a word, O king, [ret]urn to me, [lest] Milk-ilu, as well as Labʾaya, [he]ar.
