(006) [N]ow, thus I have written to the k[ing], my lord, but he does not heed my words. Since he has attacked me three times this year and (since) I have been robbed of my grain for the past two years, there is no grain for us to [e]at. What can I say to my working class? Their sons, their daughters, and the wood of their houses are (together) all depleted due to paying Yarimuta to preserve our lives.
