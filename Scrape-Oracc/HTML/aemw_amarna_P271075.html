(031) Yapaʾ-Haddi is hostile against me on account of [my] property that is in his possession. Let us present the case before Aman-[mašš]a, DUMU-piha and Yanhamu so that they will know my rights concerning my property. (It is) because my property in his possession is extensive (that) he is thus waging war against me. When I heard about [the capture] of Ullaza, I wrote [...] to him, but he was afraid of the two sons. So he took [...] [with] the two of them and he waged [war aga]inst me on account of [my] p[roperty] [...] its [e]nemy to take [grain to eat fr]om [Yarimuta, so that I would not d]ie.
