(008) Moreover, why did the king send (an army) to Aziru? He does whatever he wants! You see, Aziru assassinated Aduna, king of ʾIrqata; the king of Ammiya, the king of Ardata, and the senior official he (likewise) assassinated and took their cities for himself. Ṣumur is his, (as are) the cities of the king. Byblos alone is a vessel of the king.
