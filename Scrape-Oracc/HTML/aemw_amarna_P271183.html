(021) The king of Hatti is located in N[u]ǵasse and I am fearful because of him. May it not be (that) he comes <co>mes to Amurru. If he attacked Tunip, it is only two one-day marches to where he is located. So I am fearful because of him and on account of that matter, I am standing still until he departs.
