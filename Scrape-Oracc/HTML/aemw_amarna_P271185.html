(022) May the king, my lord, know that hostility is quite severe against me. All the cities the king placed in my control have (since) allied with the apiru men. May the king place me in control of the man that will lead the king's regular army in order to call to reckoning the cities that have allied with the apiru men, so that you can return them to my control and I may be able to serve the king, my lord, like our ancestors of old.
