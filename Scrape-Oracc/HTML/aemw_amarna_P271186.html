(007) May the king, my lord, know that the hostility of Abdi-Aširta against me is severe. He now endeavors to capture [for himself] the (final) two cities left to me. What is more, who (even) is he, [(this) Abdi-A]širta, the dog, that he endeavors to take all the cities of the king, the sun god, [fo]r himself? (Does he think) he is the king of Mittanni or the king of Babylonia that [he] endeavors to capture the land of the king for himself?
