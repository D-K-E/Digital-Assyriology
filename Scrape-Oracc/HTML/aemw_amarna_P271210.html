(018) Since it is said in the presence of the king, "Rib-Haddi has caused the death of the king's regular troops," as the commissioners live I will make known all their deeds. The king knows that I am a loyal servant of his. May the king pay non attention to the slander (against) his loyal servant that [hostile] men are speaking in the presence of [the king], my [lord].
