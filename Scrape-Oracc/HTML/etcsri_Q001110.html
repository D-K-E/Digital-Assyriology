(column 1)
i 1 1 dnin-ŋir₂-su₂

(i 1) For Ninĝirsu, Enlil’s warrior.

i 2 2 ur-saŋ! den-lilₓ(E₂)-ra
i 3 3 en-mete-na

(i 3) En-metena, ruler of Lagaš, chosen by Nanše in the heart, chief governor of Ninĝirsu, constructed a lofty levee along the border that Enlil demarcated for Ninĝirsu. For Ninĝirsu, his master who loves him, En-metena extended it from the Idnun canal until Mubikura. He erected a stela for him in the field at Ninĝirsu’s border.
