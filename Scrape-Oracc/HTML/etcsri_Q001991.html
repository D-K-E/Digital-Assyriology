Enlil-bani 09

1 1 den-lil₂-ba-ni

(1) When I, Enlil-bani, the shepherd, who makes everything abundant for Nibru, the farmer of Urim’s plentiful barley, the en priest suitable for Unug, ceaseless provider of Eridug, the spouse chosen in the heart by Inana, established justice in Sumer and Akkad, then I built the eight palaces of the kings.
