Warad-Sin 11

1 1 dinana zabala₂ki

(1) For Inana of Zabalam, my lady, for my well-being, and for the well-being of Kudur-mabuk, the father who begot me, (I), Warad-Sin, king of Larsam, built her holy high temple, a residence of warriors, I raised it as high as a mountain.
