(12) When Dugul-pan-ili went to the shearing, that person stole his (sheep) dues; he did not come in to the shearing but fled and took refuge in a temple. I sent (men) to take him down and (told him): "I will excuse your dues (but) bring the men, come and do (your) work in Dur-Šarruken."
