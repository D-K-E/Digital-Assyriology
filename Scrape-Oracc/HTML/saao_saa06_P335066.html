@translation labeled en project
@(1) 10 minas of silver by the (mina) of Carchemish, 75 rams, and one
    heifer belonging to Remanni-Adad, chariot driver,

@(4) at the disposal of Arbailayu, deputy (governor) of Barhalza,
    Nabû-riba-ahhe the scribe, Mašqaru the 'third man,' and Il-dalâ ditto.

@(8) They have taken it as a loan. It shall increase by a third.

@(10) One shall give back the sheep and the heifer in Adar (XII).
    If not, the sheep will give birth.

@(r 1) [Mon]th Tebet (X), 25th day, eponym year of Šarru-lu-dari.

@(r 2) [Wit]ness Nergal-šarru-uṣur, 'third man.'

@(r 3) Witness Uarmeri, ditto.

@(r 4) Witness Šamaš-šarru-u[ṣur], chariot driver.

@(r 5) Witness Nabû-šezib, ditto. Witness Barruqu.

@(r 6) Witness Šumma-ilani, ditto. Witness Sakkannu, ditto.

@(r 7) Witness Aššur-ila'i, cohort commander of the royal bodyguard.

@(r 8) Witness Zarutî, chariot driver of the prince.

@(r 9) Witness Zarutî, cohort commander of the prince.

@(r 10) Witness Habasti, head porter.

@(e. 1) [Witness] Nergal-šarru-uṣur, 'third man.'

@(e. 2) [Witness Marduk]-šarru-uṣur, son of Gabbî.
