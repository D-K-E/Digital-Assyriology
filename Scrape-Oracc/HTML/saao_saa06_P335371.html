(12) Whoever in the future or at any time lodges a complaint, [whe]ther Paruṭṭu or his sons, grandsons, brothers or nephews, and seeks a lawsuit or litigation [again]st Issar-duri and his sons, shall place [x tale]nts of silver and 10 minas of gold in the lap of Ištar [of] Nineveh, and shall return the silver tenfold [to] its owners. He shall contest in his lawsuit and not succeed.
