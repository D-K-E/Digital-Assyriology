@(r 1) [whoever] seeks a lawsuit or litigation against Remanni-Adad
    and his sons (and) grandsons, shall place two talents of refined silver
    and one talent of pure gold in the lap of Ištar residing in Nineveh,
    shall tie two white horses to the feet of Aššur, and shall return the
    money tenfold to its owners.

@(r 7) He shall contest in his lawsuit and not succeed.
