SAA 10 183. Extispicy on the Roof of the Temple of Marduk (ABL 1278) [from diviners]

Obverse
o 1 o 1

ina UGU eš-še-bu-u.MUŠEN šá taq-bu-u-ni

(1) Concerning the hoopoe about which you said: "It has been sent as a message" — and concerning the temple of Marduk, we shall see a good month, and it (the ritual) should be performed; he does not know.

o 2 2

ma-a* šip-ru šú-u šap-ir ina UGU É—dAMAR.UTU

o 3 3

ITI DÙG.GA né-mur ù li-in-né-piš šu-ú

(3) Elul (VI) is a good month.

o 4 4

la u-da ITI.KIN DÙG.GA ù ina UGU dul-li

(4) Also, concerning the ritual of the haruspex, he does not know about the 2nd day; again, it is good. Let it be performed and let us be confident.

(6) Also, if it is feasible, why don't the guards and Perhu keep watch there, on account of a matter like this?

(r 5) [Co]ncerning the roof of the temple of Marduk about which the king, my lord, spoke, it is good to perform (the ritual) there. Elul (VI) is a good month; and the 2nd day is suitable for extispicy. Let it be performed accordingly.
