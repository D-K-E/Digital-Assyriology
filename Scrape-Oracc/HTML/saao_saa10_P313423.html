SAA 10 381. Rituals, Prayers, and Laments Because of Mars (CT 53 008) [miscellaneous]

Obverse
1 1

NAM.BÚR.BI ḪUL DÙ.A.BI LÚ.ENGAR le-pu-uš

(1) The 'farmer' should perform the apotropaic ritual against evil of any kind; the 'farmer' should (also) perform the penitential psalms for Nergal and the 'hand-lifting' prayer for Nergal. Let them write in the apotropaic ritual and the prayer as follows:

2 2

ÉR.ŠÀ.ḪUN.GÁ-MEŠ šá dU.GUR ù šu-íl-la-ka-nu

3 3

šá dU.GUR LÚ.ENGAR le-pu-uš ina ŠÀ NAM.BÚR.BI

4 4

u šu-íl-la-ka-nu liš-šá-⸢ṭir⸣

5 5

um-ma ina ḪUL MUL.ṣal-bat-a-⸢nu⸣

(5) "In the evil of the planet Mars which exceeded its term and ap[peared] in the constellation Aries: may its evil not [approach], not come near, not press up[on (me)], not affect me, my country, the people of [my pal]ace and my army!"

6 6

ša a-dan-šú ú-še-ti-[qu-u-ni]

7 7

ù ina ŠÀ MUL.LÚ.ḪUN.GÁ ⸢in⸣-[na-mir-u-ni]
