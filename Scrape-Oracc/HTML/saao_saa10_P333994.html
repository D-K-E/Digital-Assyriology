(17) The king, my lord, did not question (the officials) who, in [...] and in the [... te]mple, fill the vats [in fr]ont of the king's table, (but) are now on strike, about whom I wrote to the king, my lord. Now, in the month Tishri (VII), they have filled neither the libation wine nor the vats in front of Aššur; neither the wine master, nor his deputy, nor his secretary! They are on strike hand in hand (lit. foot to foot). The king, my lord, should know (this).
