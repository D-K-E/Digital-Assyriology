(r 12) [As to what] the king, my lord, wrote [to me] saying that Aššur-mukin-paleya should appear before the king, my lord — [as so]on as the planet Venus has become visible, [he may] come. Venus will presently make a good omen; meanwhile the month Nisan (I) has arrived. The constellation Aries is setting and will (soon) be completely gone. The king, my lord, should still wait for its emergence; thereafter he may come. May Bel and Nabû [let] the king, my lord, [see Aššur-muk]in-pa[leya prosper].
