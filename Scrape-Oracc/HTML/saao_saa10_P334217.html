(r 11) This lunar eclipse which took place, afflicted all countries, but all its evil is heaped upon the Westland. "Westland" means the Hittite country (Syria) or, according to another interpretation, Chaldea. With the king, my lord, all is well. However, the guard should not be neglected, and the relevant apotropaic ritual should be performed for the king, my lord.
