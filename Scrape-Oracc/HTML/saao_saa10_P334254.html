SAA 10 233. Funerary Rituals (ABL 0378) [from exorcists]

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL be-lí-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant Marduk-šakin-šumi. The best of health to the king, my lord! May Nabû and Marduk bless the king, my lord! May Ištar of Arbela give happiness and health to the king, my lord! May she sate the king, my lord, with old age and fullness of life! May she present the king, my lord, with long-lasting days!
