(16) As to what the king, our lord, wrote to us: "There is more obsidian" — if 'eyes' are lacking, eye-stones should be made of it, and [if] a šanduppu (ornament) is lacking, a šanduppu-gem should be made of it. If it is extra, they may leave it unused.
