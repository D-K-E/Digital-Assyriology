(13) Subsequently I am sending off (an assortment of) drugs for fumigation. The kanaktu and nikiptu oil which I dispatched should be first dripped [into the ear], [thereafter] let them do the fumig[ation]. As soon as they have fumi[gated], they should repeat (the procedure), drip [the rest of the oil] upo[n (a tuft of) red wool and insert it] into the ear. [It is] very [efficien]t.
