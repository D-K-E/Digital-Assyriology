b.e. 18 b.e. 18

LUGAL be-lí am-ru

(18) The king, my lord, is the chosen one of the great gods; the shadow of the king, my lord, is pleasant for everything. Let them come up and run around in the sweet and pleasant shadow of the king, my lord. May the king, my lord, see them prosper, and may their grandchildren in like manner run around in the presence of the king, my lord!
