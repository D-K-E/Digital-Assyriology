SAA 10 328. Medications for the King (ABL 0740) [from physicians]

a-na LUGAL be-lí-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant Ikkaru. The very best of health to the king, my lord! May Nabû and Marduk bless the king, my lord! May Bel, Nabû and the Lady of Life give happiness and physical well-being to the king, my lord!
