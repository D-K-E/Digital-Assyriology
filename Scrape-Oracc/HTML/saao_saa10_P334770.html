SAA 10 283. Informing the King (ABL 1173) [from exorcists]

Obverse
o 1 o 1

[a-na LUGAL be-lí]-ia ARAD-ka md[AG]—⸢SUM*⸣—MU

(1) [To the king], my [lord]: your servant [Nabû]-nadin-šumi. [Good health] to the king, my lord! May Nabû and Marduk bless the king, my lord! May they give long-lasting days, ever-lasting years, happiness and physical well-being to the king, my lord! As heaven and earth last for ever, may the name of the king, my lord, last for ever in the land of Assyria!
