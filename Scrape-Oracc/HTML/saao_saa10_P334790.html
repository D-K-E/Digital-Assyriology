(19) The shepherds have (however) bribed both the commandant [and the] pr[elate]: up till now no account [of] the bulls and sheep has been made, nor have they supplied the regular ram offerings. They have not (even) sacrificed the king's offerings, the ungelded bulls, in the month Nisan (I), (but) do the bull herdsmen's bidding. They turn the blesser's ungelded bulls back from the (temple) gate, and have served (such bulls) on the table of Nabû.
