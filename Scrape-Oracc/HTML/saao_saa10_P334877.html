(r 1) May Bel and Nabû lengthen his days, and may the king, my lord, see him prosper! The month is good, this day is good: the planet Mercury (signifies) the crown prince, and it is vis[ib]le in the constellation [Ari]es; Venus [is] visible in [Bab]ylon, in the home of [his] dynasty (lit. father); and the moon will complete the day in the month Nisan (I). We count this together: it is propitious.
