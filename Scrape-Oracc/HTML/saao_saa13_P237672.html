[a-na LUGAL EN-ía] ARAD-ka mTUK-ši—DINGIR

(1) [To the king, my lord]: your servant, Rašil. May [Marduk and Zarpan]itu bless the king, my lord.

(3) I [was a dead dog], but the king, my lord, revived me. He helped me conquer [my famine and h]unger. [Now if] I had not written [to] the king, my lord, concerning the house of my lords [and ...], they would not have sought [... from] my [hands, and the king, my lord], would not have stretched out [his hand towar]d me.
