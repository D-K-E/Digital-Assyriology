(1) To the king, my lord: your servant Nabû-šumu-iddina. The very best of health to the king, my lord! May Nabû and Marduk bless the king, my lord! I am a dog, but the king, my lord, has remembered me. [May] Aššur, Bel, Nabû, Si[n, Šamaš], Ninurta, and Nergal [guard the life of the king, my lord]!
