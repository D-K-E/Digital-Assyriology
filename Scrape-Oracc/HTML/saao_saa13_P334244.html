SAA 13 076. Report to the Queen Mother about a Ritual (ABL 0368)

Obverse

a-na AMA—LUGAL EN-ia

(1) To the queen mother, my 'lord': your servant, Nergal-šarrani. Good health to the queen mother, my 'lord.' May Nabû and Marduk bless the queen mother, my 'lord.' May Tašmetu, whom you revere, take your hands. May you see 1,000 years of kingship for Esarhaddon.
