(1) To the king, my lord: your servant, Aššur-reṣuwa, priest of Ninurta. Good health to the king, my lord. May Bel, Aššur, and Ninurta bless the king, my lord. May they give to the king, my lord, long days, everlasting years, long life, and the attainment of all he strives for.

(r 4) They cut off a [... x] spans wide and 11 cubits (= 5.5 m) long, and they removed 8 silver bands from the walls. The eunuch of the king, my lord, should come and investigate.
