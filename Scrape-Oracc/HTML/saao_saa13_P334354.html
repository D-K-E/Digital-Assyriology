SAA 13 187. The King Had Mercy on his Servant (ABL 0514)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL EN-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant, Bel-iddina. Good health to the king, my lord. May Nabû and [Marduk] very greatly bless the king, my lord. May Aššur, Nikkal, and Lord-Crown, who love the name of the king, my lord, ask forever after the health of the king, my lord. May they give to the king, my lord, happiness and physical well-being.
