SAA 13 012. The Sanctuary of Mullissu is Completed (ABL 1092)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL be-lí-ia

(1) To the king, my lord, your servant, Marduk-šallim-ahhe, the one who blesses you. Good health to the king, my lord. May Aššur, Sin, Šamaš, Bel, Nabû, Ištar of Nineveh, and Ištar of Arbela very greatly bless the king, my lord. May they give to the king, my lord, long days and numerous years of happiness and physical well-being.
