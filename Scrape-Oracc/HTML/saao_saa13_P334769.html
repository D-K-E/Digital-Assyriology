SAA 13 009. No Deliveries from Talmusu on the 5th of the Month (ABL 1171)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL be-lí-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant, Marduk-šallim-ahhe, the one who blesses you. Good health to the king, my lord. May Aššur, Sin, Šamaš, Bel, Nabû, Ištar of Nineveh, and Ištar of Arbela very greatly bless the king, my lord. May they give to the king, my lord, long days and years of physical well-being.
