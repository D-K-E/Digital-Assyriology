(15) And the temple stewardess says: "When Nabû-zer-ketti-lešir returned from the Inner City, he entered the presence of Nabû-šallim-ahhe. Nabû-šallim-ahhe questioned him over and over, saying: 'From where have you come?' [He replied: 'I came] from the Inner City.' (Whereupon Nabû-šallim-ahhe) said: 'You! You! — [Wh]y do you want to die as a liar?! Produce the [...] goods in your possession and bring them to me!' Then Nabû-abu-da''in, the cook, cried out. He picked up Warrior Erra and gave it to him along with one set of clothing. That evening and the following morning, he produced 2 ...-containers. This cook, Nabû-abu-da''in, was beaten on the fifth day and died from the beatings."
