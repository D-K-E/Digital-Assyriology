SAA 13 186. The Weavers Have Not Given the Garments (SAAB 2 72)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL EN LUGAL-MEŠ [EN-ia]

(1) To the king, lord of kings, [my lord]: your servant, Aplaya, the priest of Kurba'il. Good health to the king, my lord. May Adad, Šala, and Šarrat-nakkanti

— the gods who dwell in Edurhenunna — bless the king, my lord, and keep him alive. May they give the king, my lord, happiness and physical well-being. May they grant long days and everlasting years to the king, my lord.
