SAA 14 023. Bel-duri Lends Barley and an Ox (658-VI-18) (ADD 0152)

32 ANŠE 5(bán) ŠE.PAD-MEŠ

(1) 32 homers and 5 seahs of barley and one wagon-ox belonging to the crown prince, under the control of Bel-duri, governor of the crown prince, at the disposal of Nargî of the town Balaṭu.
