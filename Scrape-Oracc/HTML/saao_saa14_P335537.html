@translation labeled en project
@(1) Seal of Remanni-Illil, son of Yatana-il, from Bit-abi-ila'i,
    owner of the field being sold.

$ ruling
$ (cylinder seal impression)
$ ruling
@(5) an estate of 2 hectares 3 decares of field, a lot,
    adjoining a wadi, adjoining Kiṣir-Aššur, adjoining the plain,
    adjoining the field of Ubru-Nabû, adjoining the field of Kiṣir-Aššur,

@(10) an estate of 7 decares of field adjoining the road
    that leads to Hašanu, adjoining the field of Gir-Aya, adjoining the field
    of Kakkullanu,

@(14) [in all] 3 hectares field by the seah of 9 'litres' [in] Bit-abi-ila'i —

@(r 1) Kakkullanu, cohort commander of the crown prince, has contracted
    and bought it from Remanni-Illil for 12 shekels of silver for a number
    of years.

@(r 6) He shall have the usufruct of the field for 3 crop years and
    3 fallow years, in all 6 years.

@(r 8) (If) he places silver upon the corn-heap,
    he shall redeem his land.

@(r 10) The land is exempt, void of straw and corn taxes.

$ ruling
@(r 11) Month Tebet (X), 7th day, eponym year of Aššur-matu-taqqin.

@(r 12) Witness Kiṣir-Aššur,
    cohort commander of the crown prince.

@(r 14) Witness Ubru-Nabû, ditto.

@(r 15) Witness Unzarhu-Aššur, ditto.

@(r 16) Witness Zizî, deputy
    of the cohort commander of the royal bodyguard.

@(r 18) Witness Gir-Aya. Witness Padî.

@(r 19) Wi[tness] Urdu-Mullissi.

@(r 20) Witness Nabû-pir'u-lešir, scribe, keeper of the contract.

@(e. 1) Witness Nabû-tariṣ.
