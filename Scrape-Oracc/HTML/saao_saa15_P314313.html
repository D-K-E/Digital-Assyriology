(2) The seal (impression) that he brought is not made like the seals of the king, my lord. I have in my possession a thousand seal(ed order)s of the king, my lord. I compared it with them, and [it] was not made like the seal of the king, my lord. I am herewith sending the seal(ed order) to the king, my lord. If it is genuine, let them send me a duplicate of the seal(ed order) which I s[en]t, I will deposit it [wi]th him, and he may go where the king, my lord, sent him to.
