SAA 15 288. Why is My Lord Silent While I Wag my Tail? (ABL 0382)

(1) To the deputy (governor), my lord: your servant Aššur-reṣiwa. Good health to my lord!

(4) Why is my lord silent (while) I wag my tail and run about like a dog? I have sent three letters to my lord. Why does my lord not consent to send an answer to (my) letter? Let my lord return me to my office. As much as I served your father, so let me now serve you!
