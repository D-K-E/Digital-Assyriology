(6) The magnates have built the fo[rt] of Minu'. They raised 1,000 homers of barley from the fort of the Luhut[eans] and stored it there. I have placed there 10 homers of salt, 16 [...s] of kudimmu salt, 30 jugs of oil, 18 jugs of naphtha, 30 bows, 20,000 [...] arrows, 10,000 arrow-shafts, 100 [...], two talents of [...] wool, and three talents of ......., and have appointed my o[ff]icial there accompanied by 100 Itu'aens and the Gurreans of the Palace.
