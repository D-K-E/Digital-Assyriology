SAA 16 105. Killed in the Enemy Country (ABL 0186)

a-na LUGAL be-lí-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant Ubru-Nabû. Good health to the king, my lord! May Bel, Nabû, Ištar of Nineveh (and) Ištar of the Kidmuru temple very greatly bless the king, my lord. May they give happiness (and) physical well-being to the king, my lord. The garrisons of the king, my lord, are well.

(14) "My father died in enemy country. The fifty men who were under his command, took twelve horses in their hands and came (back); they are (now) staying in the surroundings of Nineveh. I said to them: 'My father may be dead, but why did you leave the royal guard and come (back)?'"
