SAA 16 106. Leftovers of Nabu for the Crown Prince (ABL 0187)

Obverse

a-na DUMU—LUGAL EN-ia

(1) To the crown prince, my lord: your servant Ubru-Nabû. The best of health to the crown prince, my lord! May Bel, Nabû, Mullissu, the Lady of Kidmuri (and) Ištar of Arbela give happiness, physical well-being, (and) enjoyment of life to the crown prince, my lord.
