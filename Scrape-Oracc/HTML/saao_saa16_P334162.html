SAA 16 048. Petition on Behalf of a Tax Master (ABL 0221)

(1) To the palace scribe, my lord: your servant Tabnî. The best of health to my lord! May Nabû and Marduk bless my lord! Enter Nineveh in good spirits and see the face of Nabû (and) the face of the king in peace!
