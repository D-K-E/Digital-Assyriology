(6) As to what the king, my lord, wrote to his servant: "Ask Balasî about the robes and write me from where they will be delivered!" — I have asked Balasî (and he answered): "We will supply red wool from the palace and they will do as ordered." Moreover, I have asked Aplaya (and he said): "They will give us red wool. The weavers of Ištar of Arbela will come (and) make (fabrics) in Kurbail."
