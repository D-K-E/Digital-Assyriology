SAA 16 098. Dissolved Ordinances (ABL 0733)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL be-li-ia

(1) To the king, my lord: your servant Kabtî, the scribe whom the king, my lord, installed in the house of the palace superintendent. Good health to the king, my lord! May Aššur, the king of the gods, who dwells in Ešar[ra], bless the king, my lord. May he gi[ve] the king, [m]y [lord], long days, [nu]merous years and the satisfaction of extreme old age.
