(1) To the king, our lord: your servants Nabû-tukulti, Nabû-šumu-lešir (and) Mutakkil-Adad. Good health to the king, our lord! May Aššur (and) Šamaš bless the king, our lord.

(9) As to what the king, our lord, wrote to us: "I have ordered the magnates to do justice to you" — we have stood before them, but they have refused to render justice to the household of their lord. They have [s]old [all the servant]s of the crown prince for money and [finis]hed them up.
