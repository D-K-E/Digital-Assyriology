SAA 16 052. Food for Schoolboys (K 01273)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

[d]EN ù dGAŠAN-ia dAG dtaš-me-tum

(1) May Bel and Beltia, Nabû (and) Tašmetu, Ninurta and Gula, Nergal (and) Laṣ, these great gods, bless you, O Nabû-[...]! May they guard you, k[eep you in good health, and give you] happiness (and) physical well-being! [May they appoint] a guardian of health and vigour (to be) with yo[u]. Whatever you have [prayed] to these gods for, may they very, very quickly bes[tow it upon you].

(r 1) [I am] now [writing] to my lord, (as) from the first day on their little ones in Calah cannot go to school without funerary offering(s). May my lord keep his servants al[ive] (with) the stored grain of Sivan (III), until a new barley ration falls to my share.
