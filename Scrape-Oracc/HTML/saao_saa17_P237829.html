SAA 17 115. The Son of Yakin Prepares for the New Year’s Festival (ABL 0261)

Obverse
o 1 o 1

a-na LUGAL be-lí-i-ni

(1) To the king, our lord: your servants Nabû-šumu-lišir and Aqar-Bel-lumur. Good health to the king, our lord! Say to the king, our lord: the fortress and the armed forces of the king are well. The king, our lord, can be glad.
