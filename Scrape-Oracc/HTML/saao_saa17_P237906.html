(4) Aya-saggî and his travel companion, whom you sent here, did not come to me. Having been captured in the city centre, Aya-siggi was taken to me. He says: "I was sent to you." (However) he had no letter with him. I kept him with me until I saw your messenger. Now I have sent him to you with your messenger.
