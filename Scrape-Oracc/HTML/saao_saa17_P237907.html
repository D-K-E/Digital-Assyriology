SAA 17 063. Nobody Helps me, While You Eat and Drink (ABL 0897)

(1) Tablet of Nabû-taklak to Gadiya', his brother. Good health to my brother!

(3) As to what my brother wrote: "May you be in good spirits!" How could I be in good spirits! The enemy has pitched camp over against us, we [assemble] for fight [day and] night, and none of yo[u] has co[me] to my help! I am not in good [spirits] at all! (But) really, you must be in good spirits yourself! You live in [your] lords' house, you eat bread and drink [beer] comfortably in [the house of your lord]!
