SAA 17 128. Writing Down and Sending the King the Names of Noblemen (ABL 0900)

ARAD-ka mkal-bi—ú-⸢ku⸣-ú

(1) Your servant Kalbi-Ukû: I would gladly die for the king, my lord! Good health to the ki[ng], my lord. Say to the king, m[y] lord: I am herewith sending a tablet for the well-being of the king, my lord.
