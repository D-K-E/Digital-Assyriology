(11) I have made inqui[ries]. After he wrote to the son of Yakin, Kuttiya, the son of Šarrani, wanted to return to his presence. He did not [agree but] gave him [40] minas of silver and spoke to him with these words: "I have already given you [4]0 minas of silver. And now [I will give] you 10 minas of silver and send you back to the son of [NN]. Go and encourage the Babylonians and say to them (thus): '[Since] you went away, there has been no more procession of Nin[urta]. Because of this [...] has not come, but he will come [next year]. And I myself [...].'"
