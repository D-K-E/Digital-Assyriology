(3) [Co]ncerning Re'indu of the household of the šandabakku about whom the king, my lord, [w]rote to me, I looked for her in Babylon but did not find her (there). Having asked and made enquiries, (I found) she was living in Dilbat. I have (now) brought her in from Dilbat and am herewith [sen]ding her to the king, my lord, in the hands of Na'id-ilu, the eunuch who brought the (king's) seal(ed letter) to me.
