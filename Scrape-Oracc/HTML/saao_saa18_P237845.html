(r 2) Also, there is a gua[rd] who has ta[ken a wife] from among the Ruṣapeans. He is a Damunean; thirty years ago he married a Zanakian woman. Now he has claimed her, and I have sent him to the king, my lord. The Zanakians take wives from among the Damuneans and the Gambuleans, (and) they are all making claims. The king, my lord, should not act wrongly in this.
