SAA 18 064. Greetings from Ezida! Take Good Care of Yourself! (ABL 0219)

(1) A tablet of Bel-upaq to Kunâ, his father. Good health to my father! I pray every day to Nabû and Nanaya for the life of my father, and I steadily do (my) duty for Ezida on behalf of my father.
