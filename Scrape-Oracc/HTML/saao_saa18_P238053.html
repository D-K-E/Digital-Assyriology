(6) After the Elamites had marched against us and taken control of the bridge, when they came, I wrote to the mother of the king, my lord. Now they have dismantled the bridge, but have retained the floats of the bridge in .... They have not released them. We do not know whether or not they will go on. If they do, I shall write to the mother of the king, my lord. The forces must reach us, my lord!
