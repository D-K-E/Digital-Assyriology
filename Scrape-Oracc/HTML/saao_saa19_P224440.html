(18) We spoke many words with them, but some 10 powerful men refuse to come out and speak with us; they keep sending (messages) to us. We told them: “Open the city-gate, so we can enter Babylon.” They refused, saying: "If we let you enter Babylon, what can I say to the [k]ing, when the king himself comes?" They will open the gate (only) when [the ki]ng comes, and they do not believe that the king will come.
